LUCO

Kyle Phillips and Alice Tung

The counting procedure for the LUCO project is fairly simple. Begin by turning on a microscope and using a micrometer to standardize the measurements of the individuals in the sample. The simplest way to do this is to use the white microscope that is connected to the computer near the lab door to the outside.
