Why Count Zooplankton?

Zooplankton play a huge role in aquatic ecosystems and truly facilitate energy transport starting from primary producers like between phytoplankton up to higher trophic levels composed of larger consumers. But where there still remains a lack of understanding is how physical factors in the oceanic and estuarine environments affect how much and what kind of zooplankton live in certain areas. Even more unknown is how recent anthropogenic activity-driven environmental changes have impacted zooplankton populations and in turn affect the greater food webs. By understanding population dynamics of zooplankton communities over time, we can better understand the health of individual ecosystems in terms of abundance, diversity, and production of plankton and nekton.
