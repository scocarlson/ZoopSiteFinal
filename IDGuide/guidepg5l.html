Looks like you have a Simocephalus sp. Cladoceran.

Defining characteristics of Simocephalus sp. are:

  • Ovular, translucent body
  • Visible eyespot
  • Smaller, conical head
  • Absence of a posterior spine

If a Simocephalus sp. is gravid, it will have one or more eggs sacs in its abdominal region. Learn More.
