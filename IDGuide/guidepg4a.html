Looks like you have a Limnothona sp.

Defining characteristics of Limnothona sp. are:

  • Teardrop body morphology
  • Short first antennae
  • Long urosome
  • Relatively smaller body size compared to Acanthocyclops sp.

If a Limnothona is gravid, it will have two eggs sacs on either side of its body. Learn More.
