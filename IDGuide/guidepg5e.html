Looks like you have a Eurytemora sp. Copepod.

Defining characteristics of Eurytemora sp. are:

  • Sausage-like body morphology
  • Mid-length first antennae
  • Deep split in the urosome

If your specimen has a fully developed fifth leg or is visibly gravid, then it is an adult individual. If these attributes are not apparent, then it is a copepodite. Learn More.
