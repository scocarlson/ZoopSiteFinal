Looks like you have a Calanoid Copepod.

If your specimen has a sausage-like body and mid-length antennae, it is probably either a Pseudodiaptomus forbesi or a Eurytemora affinis If it has a cigar-like body and antennae that extend longer than the length of its body, it is most likely an Acartiella sinensis or a Sinocalanous doerrii.

  • Sausage-like body morphology
  • Mid-length antennae (~length of prosome)
  • Cigar-like body morphology
  • Long antennae (longer than length of prosome)
