Great! Looks like you have a Daphnia magna.

If there are eggs in its abdomen, then it is a gravid female. If it has two small cylindrical appendages protruding from its rostrum, it is an adult male. If neither of those attributes are visible, it should just be classified as a female adult Daphnia magna. Learn More
