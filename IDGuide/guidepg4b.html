Looks like you have an Acanthocyclops sp. Copepod.

Defining characteristics of Acanthocyclops sp. are:

  • Teardrop body morphology
  • Short first antennae
  • Short urosome
  • Relatively larger body size compared to Limnothona sp.

If your specimen has a fifth leg, it is an adult. If not, it is a copepodite. If an Acanthocyclops sp. is gravid, it will have two eggs sacs on either side of its body. Learn More.
