Acartiella sp.

Defining characteristics are:

  • Cigar-like body morphology
  • Long antennae (longer than length of prosome)
  • Absence of distal spikes
  • Smoother, tapered body morphology

Does your specimen have a fully developed fifth leg? If so, you have an adult Acartiella sp. If not, your specimen is a copepodite. Learn More.
