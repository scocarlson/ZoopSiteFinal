Your specimen may be a less common local species of Cladocera.

If your specimen has enlarged second antennae, it is most likely a Diphanosoma sp. If it has sloped head and a small dangling appendage right next to its second antennae, it is likely a Moina sp.

  • Enlarged second antennae
  • Conical head
  • Small dangling appendage next to second antennae
  • Slightly sloped head
