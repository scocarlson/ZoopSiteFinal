Looks like you have a either a Sinocalanous doerrii or an Acartiella sinensis Copepod.

If your specimen has two small spikes on either end of its distal prosome and a boxy body morphology, it is most likely a S. doerrii If it has a smoothly tapered body morphology and no spikes, it is likely an A. sinensis.

  • Presence of distal spikes
  • Boxy body morphology
  • Absence of distal spikes
  • Smoother, tapered body morphology
