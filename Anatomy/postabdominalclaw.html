Postabdominal Claw

The postabdominal claw is an attribute shared between all Cladocera and it is located in the inferior portion of the carapace. The size of the postabdominal claw is directly porportional to an individual's size. The postabdomal claw serves as a key factor in identifying between Daphnia magna or Daphnia pulex in that Daphnia magna have a divet in the claw while Daphnia pulex do not.
