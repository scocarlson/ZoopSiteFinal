Eye

Though not technically a traditional "eye", the eyespot that both cladocera and copepoda have located on their superior prosome is a a heavily pigmented region that functions as a light receptor. A major component of zooplankton diet is plant matter which needs sunlight to photosyntheszie. These eyespots may serve as a guide for zooplankton to move toward areas where food is available.
