Prosome

The prosome makes up the majority of body mass in copepods and is the main body segment from which the legs, the first antennae, and the urosome are attached. The most distinctive differences between each commonly found copepod species is the prosome shape; teardrop-like for Acanthocyclops sp. and Limnothona sp., sausage-like for Pseudodiaptomus sp. and Eurytemora sp., and cigar-like for Acartiella sp. and Sinocalanous sp.
