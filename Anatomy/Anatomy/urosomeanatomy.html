Urosome

The urosome is located inferior to the prosome and includes the genital somite and the anal somite which are the segments were the genitals and the anus are located, respectfully. In some calanoid species, the location in which the urosome splits into rami is significant for identification purposes. In Eurytemora the split is deep while in Pseudodiaptomus the split is much shallower. In adult individuals, it is much easier to identify between the two calanoid species, but in copepodites, it is difficult to accurately classify the two.
