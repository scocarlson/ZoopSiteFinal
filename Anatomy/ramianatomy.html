Rami

The rami are the two distal appendages the urosome splits into in copepods. This is a signifcant area for the purpose of identification between calanoid copepods, Eurytemora and Pseudodiaptomus. In Eurytemora, the rami begin halfway through the urosome while Pseudodiaptomus rami begin 3/4 through the urosome.
