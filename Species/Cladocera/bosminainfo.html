Bosmina

Bosmina is a very common cladoceran species in the Delta, recognizable by its round body morphology and first antennae. Bosmina look similar to Chydorids in that both species have a circular body shape, but a Bosmina has a long first antennae extending from its rostrum while a Chydorid does not. Like most other cladoceran, Bosmina are more likely to be found in a body of slow moving water like Wings Landing or other pond sites.
