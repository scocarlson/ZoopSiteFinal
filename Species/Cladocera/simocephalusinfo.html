Simocephalus

Simocephalus is a less common Cladoceran species in the Delta and is recognizable by its ovular body morphology, lack of a posterior tail spine, and smaller, conical head. Like most other cladoceran, Simocephlaus is more likely to be found in a body of slow moving water like Wings Landing or other pond sites.
