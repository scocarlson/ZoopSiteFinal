Collembola

Collembola, also known as Springtails, compose the largest of three lineages of hexpods that are no longer considered insects. They share some characteristics of insects includings a head, thorax, and abdomen with six legs. Collembola are commonly mistaken as fleas, but do not generally bite or harm humans.
