Ostracoda

Ostracoda are a class of Crustacea commonly found in the Delta. These organisms are easily distinguishable by their dark pigment and bean-like body morphology. There are three genera found in the Delta: Eucypris, Ilyocypris, and Cypridopsis. Though Ilyocypris and Cypridopsis are rarely sighted,Eucypris is quite common and can be identified by the small cilia emerging from the edge of the organism.
