Nematoda

Commonly parasitic to humans, Nematoda is a phylum of bilaterally symmetrical roundworms commonly found in the Delta. Nematodes are one of the most abundant creatures on Earth and occcupy a wide variety of environments. One unique characteristic about organisms in this phylum is their simple body plan and their complete lack of cilia or flagella.
